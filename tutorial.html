How to Use

1

Create Your Account

Register to start organizing your resources. Click the "Register" button in the navigation.

2

Add Your First Resource

Click "Add Link" to save a URL or upload a file. The system will automatically analyze and categorize it.

3

Organize & Browse

Visit "Groups" to see your organized resources. Use tags and categories for better organization.

4

Search & Share

Use the search feature to find resources. Create collections to share with others.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of resources can I save?

URLs, documents (PDF, DOC), images, and more. The system automatically processes and indexes the content.

How does auto-categorization work?

Our AI analyzes content and metadata to automatically assign relevant categories and tags.

Can I collaborate with others?

Yes! Create shared collections and invite others to contribute or view your resources.
